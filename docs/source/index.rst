Understanding the SSO ID Framework
----------------------------------
The concept of Single Sign-On is simple yet powerful. Traditionally, a citizen would need different credentials for paying electricity bills, applying for a government job, or checking land records. The `SSO ID <https://ssoidrajasthann.com>`_ framework consolidates these services. 

Once you log in to the portal, the system creates a secure session that allows you to jump between different departmental applications without re-authenticating. This is achieved through secure token exchange protocols, ensuring that your data remains private while providing maximum convenience.

Key Benefits of the Rajasthan SSO Portal
----------------------------------------
1. **Unified Dashboard:** Access over 100+ government apps from a single screen.
2. **Data Synchronization:** If you update your profile in the SSO system, it reflects across all connected departments like e-Mitra and Jan Aadhaar.
3. **Enhanced Security:** The portal uses Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) and is linked with Aadhaar/Jan Aadhaar to prevent identity theft.
4. **Time Efficiency:** No more physical visits to government offices. Everything from scholarship applications to business licenses can be done online.

Detailed Step-by-Step Registration Guide
----------------------------------------
Registering for an SSO ID  is a one-time process. Depending on your status, you can choose one of the following three categories:

**1. For Citizens (Nagarik)**
This is the most common category for individuals.
* **Via Jan Aadhaar:** This is the most recommended method. The system fetches your family details automatically, ensuring a pre-verified profile.
* **Via Aadhaar (UID):** Use your 12-digit Aadhaar number. An OTP will be sent to your registered mobile number for instant verification.
* **Via Google Account:** You can use your Gmail ID for initial signup, though you will eventually need to link your Aadhaar for full service access.

**2. For Industries (Udhyog)**
Business owners can register using their **BRN (Business Registration Number)** or Udyog Aadhaar. This allows companies to apply for various industrial subsidies and environmental clearances.

**3. For Government Employees**
State employees use their **SIPF (State Insurance and Provident Fund)** ID to access departmental tools, leave management, and payroll services.



Common Services Available via SSO ID
------------------------------------
Once you have your SSO ID , you can access a vast ecosystem of services:

* **e-Mitra:** The most widely used service for paying utility bills (water, electricity), applying for caste certificates, and domicile documents.
* **Jan Aadhaar:** Manage your family’s social security benefits and link them to your bank accounts for direct benefit transfers (DBT).
* **Recruitment Portal:** All Rajasthan state government job applications (RPSC, RSSB) are managed through this portal.
* **Scholarship Portal:** Students can apply for various state and central scholarships directly.
* **HTE (Higher & Technical Education):** For college admissions and fee payments.

Managing Your SSO Profile
-------------------------
It is vital to keep your profile updated. If you change your mobile number or address, you should update it in the SSO dashboard immediately. Since many services use the data from your profile to auto-fill forms, an outdated profile can lead to application rejections.

Security Best Practices
-----------------------
To keep your SSO ID  secure, follow these guidelines:
* **Never share your OTP:** Government officials will never ask for your OTP or password.
* **Use a Strong Password:** Combine uppercase letters, numbers, and symbols.
* **Logout Properly:** Especially when using public computers or e-Mitra kiosks, always ensure you have logged out of the session.

Troubleshooting Common Issues
-----------------------------
**What to do if you forget your SSO ID?**
If you forget your username, visit the login page and click on "Forgot My Digital Identity (SSOID)". You can recover it by sending an SMS: **RJ SSO** to **9223166166** from your registered mobile number.

**How to reset a forgotten password?**
Click the "Forgot Password" link. You will be prompted to enter your SSO ID and choose a verification method (Mobile OTP, Email OTP, or Biometric).

**Account is Locked?**
An account usually gets locked after 3 or more failed login attempts. This is a security feature. Usually, it unlocks automatically after 24 hours, but you can also contact the helpdesk for immediate assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
--------------------------------

**Q: Is it mandatory to have a Jan Aadhaar for SSO registration?**
A: While not strictly mandatory for registration (you can use Google), it is required to access 90% of the services like scholarships and subsidies.

**Q: Can I have more than one SSO ID?**
A: No. One citizen should only have one SSO ID  linked to their Aadhaar. Multiple IDs can cause issues with government benefit distributions.

**Q: How much does it cost to register?**
A: The registration process is 100% free. If an e-Mitra center charges you, it should only be for their service time, not for the ID itself.
